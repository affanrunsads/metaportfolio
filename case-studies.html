E-Commerce

Scaling E-Commerce Brand to 5.2x ROAS in 45 Days

Niche: Premium Fashion & Accessories | Target Market: USA & UAE

5.2x ROAS
$145K REVENUE

The Challenge

The brand suffered from heavy ad fatigue, unstructured conversion funnels, and overlapping audiences. CPA hovered at $32.00, generating a razor-thin ROAS of 1.5x, rendering high-spend scaling impossible.

Affan's Scaling Blueprint

  • Consolidated Campaign Architecture: Merged fragmented ad sets into simplified Advantage+ shopping campaigns to streamline Meta's learning phase.
  • Creative Hook Variations: Designed and deployed vertical videos matching the hook-to-hold benchmark, reducing CPC from $1.40 to $0.55.
  • Dynamic Broad Targeting: Unlocked scaling bottlenecks by feeding clean pixel signal to broad demographic stacks rather than narrow target circles.

SaaS & Lead Generation

Generating $0.15 High-Intent Software Trial Signups

Service: B2B productivity web app | Target Market: USA, UK, Canada

$0.15 COST PER SIGNUP
4.5% CTR

The Challenge

Competing with massive tech giants meant high bidding costs in Facebook auctions. Trial acquisition CPL was stuck at an unsustainable $3.50, and trial-to-paid conversions were low due to low lead intent.

Affan's Scaling Blueprint

  • Value-First Hook Architecture: Created quick, problem-solving reels showcasing real dashboard workflows to target active user frustration.
  • Pre-Qualification Funnels: Added a micro-interactive survey page before the sign-up form, weeded out passive clickers and dramatically boosting lead intent.
  • Custom Watch-Time Retargeting: Retargeted users who completed 50%+ of our educational reels, converting warm leads for pennies.

Info Products / eBooks

eBook Launch Generating 12x ROAS & Sub-$0.02 CPR

Product: Paid educational templates and resources | Target Market: Southeast Asia & LATAM

12.4x ROAS
$0.018 CPR

The Challenge

Launching an eBook in competitive developing markets where purchasing power requires highly optimized, cost-efficient setups. High click prices ($0.15+) immediately squeezed profit margins on low-ticket offers.

Affan's Scaling Blueprint

  • Ultra low cost-per-click funnel: Designed custom click-to-messenger sequences that bypassed traditional high-overhead landing pages.
  • Dynamic A/B hook structure: Scaled high-CTR video overlays (tested up to 11% CTR), creating massive viral reach and securing organic ad distribution.
  • Lookalike stacking: Cloned hyper-converting customer groups to scale ad spend without facing auction competition.
